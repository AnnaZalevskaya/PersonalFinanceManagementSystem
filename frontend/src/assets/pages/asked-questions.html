Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new financial operation?

To add a new financial operation to the personal finance management system, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Select the account you are interested in.
  3. Click the "Add Operation" button.
  4. Fill in the required fields such as amount, category and description.
  5. Click the "Save" button to add a financial operation.
